NDCG (Normalized DCG)
47.06%
Ranking quality metric

NDCG (Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain) measures the quality of the ranking by considering both the position and relevance of retrieved documents. It's calculated as DCG/IDCG, where DCG gives more weight to relevant documents at higher positions (discounted by log of position), and IDCG is the ideal maximum DCG. Values closer to 100% indicate better ranking quality. NDCG is particularly useful for evaluating how well the most important documents are ranked.
